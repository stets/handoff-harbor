Wix handoffs get messy when the client can edit everything but owns nothing clearly.

Wix is beginner-friendly, which is exactly why clients often assume every future tweak is simple. The risky parts are usually hidden: who owns the account, which email receives form leads, who pays the premium plan, what apps renew, where DNS lives, and who is responsible when a client edit breaks a layout.

This is not legal, accounting, or platform policy advice. It is a practical closeout checklist for freelance designers and small studios so a Wix launch ends with ownership, access, and support expectations in writing.

What to document before a Wix project is accepted

Account, site, and billing ownership

Confirm the client controls the Wix account or site ownership, premium plan, billing email, renewal date, recovery options, 2FA, and contributor roles.

Domains, DNS, email, and launch settings

Record registrar ownership, Wix nameservers or DNS records, SSL status, connected domains, redirects, email records, favicon, and publish workflow.

Forms, apps, bookings, and automations

List contact form recipients, CRM destinations, automations, Wix Apps, bookings/events, payments, subscriptions, invoices, API keys, embeds, and renewal costs.

Editing and support boundaries

Separate client-safe edits from paid changes: redesigns, new pages, app setup, booking/payment troubleshooting, SEO work, forms, automations, and mobile layout fixes.

Copy/paste Wix handoff checklist

  • ☐ Client controls the correct Wix account/site ownership, recovery email, 2FA, premium plan, and billing responsibility.
  • ☐ Site roles, contributor access, agency access, and old contractor access have been reviewed and unnecessary permissions removed.
  • ☐ Domain registrar, connected domains, nameservers/DNS records, SSL status, email records, and renewal dates are documented.
  • ☐ Contact forms are tested; notification recipients, Wix Inbox/CRM handling, spam settings, and follow-up automations are documented.
  • ☐ Wix Apps, bookings, events, ecommerce, subscriptions, payment settings, tax/shipping basics, and renewal costs are listed if used.
  • ☐ Analytics, Search Console, pixels, SEO basics, redirects, sitemap/robots status, and conversion checks are transferred or documented.
  • ☐ Mobile layout, menus, buttons, forms, checkout/booking flows, and important browser/device checks are complete.
  • ☐ Client editing notes explain which sections are safe to edit and which changes should route back to the designer.
  • ☐ Known issues, accepted tradeoffs, unsupported features, and future recommendations are written down.
  • ☐ Included support window is stated, and new pages, redesign work, SEO, app setup, automations, booking/payment fixes, or troubleshooting routes to paid support.

Wix handoff record template

Wix website handoff record Client: [client name] Website URL: [domain] Wix site/account: [site name + account owner] Launch date: [date] Prepared by: [your name] Ownership and billing: - Wix account/site owner: [client email / pending] - Premium plan and renewal date: [details] - Billing owner and payment method responsibility: [client / agency / pending] - Recovery email and 2FA owner: [client / pending] - Active roles/contributors: [list] Launch and operations: - Domain registrar/DNS owner: [details] - Connected domains and SSL status: [details] - Email records: [MX/SPF/DKIM/DMARC notes] - Publish/version history notes: [details] Features and integrations: - Forms and notification recipients: [details] - Wix Apps/bookings/events/ecommerce: [details] - Automations/CRM/newsletter routes: [details] - Analytics/Search Console/pixels: [details] - Custom code, embeds, API keys: [details] Support boundary: - Included launch support ends: [date] - Included support covers: [bug fixes tied to delivered scope] - Excluded/new work: [new pages, redesigns, app setup, booking/payment issues, SEO, automations, troubleshooting] - Ongoing support option: [care plan / hourly / not offered]

Short Wix handoff email

Subject: Wix site handoff + support boundaries Hey [Name] — the Wix site is ready for final handoff. Before we call the project accepted, I want to make sure the operating pieces are clear: Wix account/site ownership, premium plan billing, domains/DNS/email records, forms, apps, bookings/ecommerce if used, automations, analytics, mobile checks, and the support window. Please review the handoff notes below by [date]. Launch bugs tied to the original scope are covered through [support end date]. New pages, redesigns, app setup, SEO work, booking/payment changes, automation changes, or ongoing maintenance can be handled through a separate change request or care plan.
